About Me

I am a sixth-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. And I am fortunate to have two great advisors, Prof. Kang G. Shin, and Prof. Mosharaf Chowdhury.

My research interests are broadly in computer systems and networking, especially in operating systems, virtualization, and resource management. Recently, I have been working on memory disaggregation with low-latency networks, and system support for deep learning applications.
